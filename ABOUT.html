About me..

SPORTS LIFE

My sports journey has been deeply rooted in my passion for cycling, which has driven me to strive for excellence on and off the track. As a cyclist from Odisha, India, I am fortunate to be coached by Mr. Manoranjan Barik generl secratary of BDCA (Bhadrak Dist Cycling Association), who has been instrumental in honing my skills and guiding my training. The support and inspiration from my coach and family , including my father Sarat Chandra Pohi, my mother Mamata Rani Behera, and my sister Shreshtha Shreyasi Pohi, have kept me motivated throughout my journey. Cycling has not only taught me discipline and resilience but also given me an avenue to pursue my goals with dedication and commitment.

College Life

I embarked on my Computer Science Engineering journey at Lovely Professional University, where I gained a solid foundation in software development, data structures, and problem-solving. Through hands-on projects and real-world applications, I honed my technical skills and developed a strong passion for technology. This experience was pivotal in shaping my aspiration to become a web developer.


School life

My academic journey began with my 10th grade at SBD International School, where I built a foundation in essential subjects and developed a love for learning. Later , at Bhadrak Higher Secondary School in the PCM stream, I immersed myself in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. This time was transformative, as the rigorous coursework and inspiring teachers nurtured my analytical skills and solidified my interest in engineering, setting me on the path toward a career in technology.
